Welcome to my Physics Simulation Playground!

This Python-based program offers an immersive and interactive environment for exploring the fascinating world of physics simulations.

With this versatile tool, you can create and manipulate systems of interconnected points and springs, allowing you to experiment with various configurations and observe the dynamic behaviors of these mechanical systems in real-time.

My program provides an intuitive graphical user interface (GUI) featuring sliders and buttons that enable you to adjust key parameters such as weight, rest length, and spring constant. You can even switch between different edit modes, including adding or removing springs, to fine-tune your simulations.

The visual representation of points and springs is designed to be informative, with colors indicating properties and stress levels. Whether you're a student, educator, or simply curious about physics, my Physics Simulation Playground offers an engaging way to learn and explore the principles of mechanics.
